About Night Owl Landing

Originally a Douglas Fir tree farm owned by Pope & Talbot timber company, this property has been transformed into a stunning private forest sanctuary. Since 2016, the current owners have developed miles of trails, built custom structures, and created a unique Pacific Northwest retreat for outdoor adventure and family gatherings.

With 122 acres of diverse forestland including Western Red Cedar, Big Leaf Maple, Black Cottonwood, and Red Alder trees, plus two large ponds and Silver Creek running through the property to Puget Sound, this is a rare opportunity to own your family's private wilderness estate—perfect for nature-loving families seeking adventure, privacy, and connection to the Pacific Northwest's natural beauty.
